# About Me

Hello! I'm **Fariba Sarhangnia**, an Applied AI researcher with over **7 years of experience** in **machine learning**, **deep learning**, **optimization**, and **biomedical data modeling**. I am passionate about leveraging AI to solve complex challenges in **healthcare**, **bioinformatics**, and **network analysis**, focusing on creating **explainable, interpretable, and data-driven solutions** that can have a tangible impact on both research and clinical practice. My work spans **link prediction, network analysis, multi-modal biomedical data integration**, and the development of **transparent AI models** to support informed decision-making in biomedical and engineering applications.

Throughout my career, I have collaborated with interdisciplinary teams on AI and data science projects, contributed to **5+ peer-reviewed publications**, and mentored students on deep learning and computational research, many of which have resulted in **conference presentations and publications**. I am also experienced in designing reproducible experiments, feature attribution studies, and cross-dataset generalization analyses. Beyond my research, I enjoy **teaching, mentoring, and organizing workshops**, sharing knowledge with students and professionals, as well as pursuing **mountaineering, travel, and other experiences that foster leadership, resilience, and cross-cultural adaptability**.
